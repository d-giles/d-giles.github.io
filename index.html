I'm a Ph.D. Candidate at the Illinois Institute of Technology.

I'm from Issaquah, Washington, a graduate from Westminster College in New Wilmington, Pennsylvania, and I'm currently doing my Ph.D. in Physics. I'm pursuing this degree at the Illinois Institute of Technology in Chicago, Illinois in collaboration with the Adler Planetarium where I do my research, advised by Dr. Lucianne Walkowicz.

My current research is focused on finding the most interesting (read: anomalous) variables and transients in large astronomical datasets, which can be hidden in the sheer volume of data. On any given day you'll find my nose buried in code, fine-tuning machine learning algorithms and despairing at the bugs I've accidentally hidden throughout.
